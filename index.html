Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How does the AI English Assistant work?

Our AI English Assistant uses advanced natural language processing to understand and evaluate your English. It can correct grammar, suggest improvements, help with writing, and even engage in conversation practice. The more you use it, the better it understands your learning needs and personalizes the feedback.

Are the IELTS and TOEFL materials up to date?

Yes, all our test preparation materials are regularly updated to reflect the latest exam formats and question types. We monitor official IELTS and TOEFL updates and adjust our content within 48 hours of any changes. Our practice tests mirror the actual exam experience closely.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our platform, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Premium subscribers receive certificates of completion for our structured courses. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn or included in your professional portfolio. They verify your achievement and the skills you've mastered.
