About Zakhourani Developers

We are a team of linguists, educators, and software engineers dedicated to building tools that bridge cultures and empower learners. Our projects include multilingual dictionaries, language learning platforms, academic publishing, and AI assistants like this one.

Dr. Waleed Hussein Zakhourani – our founder – is a distinguished linguist and visionary educator. A Harvard‑educated scholar, he has devoted his life to making English accessible while preserving Arabic and Kurdish heritage. His JIHAN Universal Dictionary is a landmark work for the people of Iraq and Kurdistan.

This AI assistant reflects our mission: to provide accurate, concise, and culturally aware information to everyone, everywhere.
