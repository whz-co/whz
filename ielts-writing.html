📊 Task 1: Describing a chart

20 minutes · 150 words minimum

The chart shows international student percentages in four Canadian universities. Summarise the information.

Sample (band 8.5)

The bar chart illustrates the proportion of overseas students enrolled at four Canadian universities between 2015 and 2025. Overall, all institutions saw a steady rise, with Toronto consistently highest. In 2015, UBC had around 12%, while Toronto already exceeded 18%. By 2021, McGill increased sharply to nearly 28%. Projections for 2025 suggest Toronto will reach 35%, followed by McGill and UBC. Alberta is expected to lag at 18%.

📝 Task 2: Opinion essay

40 minutes · 250 words minimum

Some people believe that unpaid community service should be a compulsory part of high school programs. To what extent do you agree or disagree? Give reasons and examples.

Sample (band 8.0)

I strongly agree that making community service compulsory in high school yields significant benefits. Firstly, it fosters civic responsibility; teenagers develop empathy through local projects. Studies show students involved in mandatory volunteering are 40% more likely to continue charitable work later. Secondly, such programs build soft skills like teamwork and communication. Admittedly, opponents claim coercion may breed resentment, but offering flexible, meaningful options often leads to genuine commitment. In conclusion, well-structured community service shapes well-rounded individuals.

✉️ General Training: Letter

20 minutes · 150 words

You have recently moved to a new city and joined a sports club. Write a letter to the club manager. In your letter: introduce yourself, explain why you joined, and ask for information about upcoming events.

Sample (band 7.5)

Dear Manager, I am writing as a new member who recently joined your sports club after moving to Manchester. I was impressed by the facilities and friendly atmosphere. I would be grateful if you could send me details about any social events or tournaments planned for the next month. I am particularly interested in badminton and swimming. Thank you for your help. Yours sincerely, Alex Chen.

📚 IELTS writing lessons

Task 1 overview

Always include an overview. Paraphrase the question, describe main trends, no detail list.

Cohesion

Use 'furthermore', 'however', 'consequently' — but vary them naturally.

Task 2 structure

Intro → 2-3 body paragraphs → conclusion. PEE: point, explanation, example.

Complex sentences

Mix simple & complex. Use relative clauses, conditionals, passive.

Time mgmt

Task 1: 20 min (150 words). Task 2: 40 min (250 words). Leave 5 min to check.

Ideas bank

Practice topics: education, environment, tech, health. Build vocab.
